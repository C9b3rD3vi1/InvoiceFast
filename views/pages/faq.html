How does the M-Pesa integration actually work?
When a client opens your invoice and clicks "Pay with M-Pesa," InvoiceFast triggers an STK Push via the Safaricom Daraja API. The client receives a USSD payment prompt on their phone, enters their M-Pesa PIN, and payment is confirmed in seconds. No manual paybill numbers, no screenshots, no back-and-forth. If the STK fails, we show the paybill number as fallback with 3 automatic retries. Your dashboard updates in real time and a KRA-compliant receipt is auto-generated instantly.
Is InvoiceFast compliant with KRA requirements?
Yes. Every receipt generated by InvoiceFast includes a scannable QR code for verification. We maintain a 7-year immutable audit trail for all invoices and payments with access logs. e-TIMs integration (KRA's electronic Tax Invoice Management System) is in active development and scheduled for Q3 2026. VAT calculations are automatic based on your registration status, and all data retention meets KRA compliance requirements.
Which payment methods do you support?
We support M-Pesa (STK Push, C2B, B2C via Daraja API), Flutterwave (cards, bank transfer, mobile money across 20+ African countries), Stripe (international cards in USD/EUR/GBP), and PayPal (global coverage). You can accept multiple partial payments against a single invoice and set up automated installment plans. All gateways are available from the Pro plan upwards.
What currencies can I invoice in?
InvoiceFast supports 7 currencies: KES (Kenyan Shilling), USD (US Dollar), EUR (Euro), GBP (British Pound), TZS (Tanzanian Shilling), UGX (Ugandan Shilling), and NGN (Nigerian Naira). Exchange rates are auto-updated daily from the Central Bank of Kenya. You can also manually override rates for specific invoices. Dual display shows both the invoice currency and KES equivalent.

How do late fees and payment reminders work?
The standard reminder sequence on Pro plans: invoice sent (Day 0), gentle reminder if not viewed (Day 3), payment due soon (Day 7), first overdue notice (Day 1 overdue), second notice + late fee warning (Day 7 overdue), final notice + account hold (Day 14 overdue), collections escalation (Day 30 overdue). Late fees can be configured as a percentage or flat fee with a grace period and maximum cap. Custom sequences are user-defined.

What happens to my data if I cancel?
You own your data — always. If you cancel, you have 30 days to export everything (invoices, clients, payment history) in CSV or PDF format. For KRA compliance, we retain invoice records for the legally required 7-year period even after account closure, and you can request access at any time. No lock-in, no data hostage.
