How does the M-Pesa integration actually work?
When a client opens your invoice and clicks "Pay with M-Pesa," InvoiceFast triggers an STK Push via the Safaricom Daraja API. The client receives a payment prompt on their phone, enters their M-Pesa PIN, and payment is confirmed in seconds. No manual sending of paybill numbers, no screenshots, no back-and-forth. Your dashboard updates in real time and a receipt is auto-generated instantly.
Is InvoiceFast compliant with KRA requirements?
Yes. Every receipt generated by InvoiceFast includes a scannable QR code for verification. We maintain a 7-year immutable audit trail for all invoices and payments. e-TIMs integration (KRA's electronic Tax Invoice Management System) is in active development and scheduled for Q3 2026. VAT calculations are automatic based on your registration status.
Which payment methods do you support?
M-Pesa (STK Push, C2B, B2C), Flutterwave (cards, bank transfer, mobile money across 20+ African countries), Stripe (international cards in USD/EUR/GBP), and PayPal. You can accept multiple partial payments against a single invoice, and set up automated installment plans. All gateways are available from the Pro plan upwards.
Can I use my own branding on invoices?
Yes, from the Pro plan. Upload your logo, choose brand colours, select a Google Font, and add a custom footer. Agency and Enterprise plans support full white-labelling — your own custom domain (e.g., invoices.yourcompany.com), no InvoiceFast branding anywhere, and a fully branded client portal.
How does the developer API work?
InvoiceFast provides a fully documented REST API available at docs.invoice.simuxtech.com. Authentication uses API keys with JWT. Webhook events (invoice.paid, invoice.overdue, invoice.viewed, payment.failed) deliver real-time updates to your systems. Available on Pro plans and above. Zapier integration gives access to 5,000+ apps without code.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
You own your data. If you cancel, you have 30 days to export everything (invoices, clients, payment history) in CSV or PDF format. For KRA compliance, we retain invoice records for the legally required 7-year period even after account closure, and you can request access at any time. No lock-in.
